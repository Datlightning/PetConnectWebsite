We are PetConnect

Pet Connect aspires to enrich the lives of pets and their owners by harnessing cutting-edge technology. Recognizing the pivotal role pets play as integral family members, our overarching goal is to seamlessly integrate innovative solutions that elevate communication, promote overall well-being, and ensure the global safety of pets. Through unwavering dedication to leveraging the latest advancements, we strive to achieve a meaningful and enriching bond between pets and their human companions, working towards the realization of a harmonious and secure environment for all.

"At Pet Connect, our mission is to enrich the lives of both pets and their owners through cutting-edge technology. We believe that pets are not just animals; they are cherished members of our families. Our purpose is to create seamless and intuitive solutions that enhance communication, improve well-being, and ensure the safety of pets worldwide."
- CEO Akshay Dhawan
